About
Hi, I am a Research Scientist at Nvidia GEAR Lab, working on creating robot foundation models for humanoids. My main research is focused on learning robot actions from videos (egocentric human videos and synthetic, neural-generated videos). I am also a Ph.D. Student at the Allen School at the University of Washington, co-advised by Luke Zettlemoyer and Dieter Fox. I was previously a research intern at Nvidia GEAR Lab working with Scott Reed, Jim Fan, and Yuke Zhu, at Nvidia Robotics Lab working with Ajay Mandlekar and at Ai2 working with Yejin Choi. I obtained an M.S. in Artificial Intelligence at KAIST AI, where I was fortunate to be advised by Minjoon Seo. Before that, I obtained a B.S. in Computer Science at Korea University.
