About
Hi, I am Guijin Son, an undergraduate researcher at Yonsei University (UIC, Economics) focusing on Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models — especially evaluation, multilingual reasoning, and LLM-as-a-judge reliability. My recent work includes Korean MMLU-style evaluation, multi-task inference, and financial NLP.

Selected profiles: Google Scholar · OpenReview · Semantic Scholar
