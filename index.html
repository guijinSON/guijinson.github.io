About

Hello. I am Guijin Son, co-founder at OneLineAI and lead of HAE-RAE, a nonprofit open source research group. I work on AI for Science, with a focus on evaluation and reasoning with language models. My current goal is to build stronger reasoning models and the measurements that prove real progress.

I design Korean evaluation benchmarks to track reasoning improvements, and I study multimodal reasoning and agentic systems. Recent projects include Korean knowledge and professional benchmarks, multi-task inference, and analysis of reward models.

I also teach and mentor. I have lectured at Fast Campus and CODED, taught with Samsung SAFI, and mentored at Upstage.
